Biography

I obtained my B.Eng. degree with honor at Zhejiang University in 2021. Currently I am a Ph.D student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

Research Interests

My research interests include:

  • In-situ and operando experiments under X-ray micro-CT

  • Fault evolution and earthquake mechanics

  • Signal processing of acoustic emission

  • Numerical modelling of geotechnical engineering

  • Underground energy storage
